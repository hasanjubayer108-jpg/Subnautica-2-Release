## 🧩 Base Building Basics

Building a base keeps you safe from the harsh ocean currents. First, find a flat area on the sea floor. Use your builder tool to place walls, rooms, and hatches. You need power to keep your base functional. Build solar panels near the surface or thermal plants near heat vents to generate electricity. Connect hallways to expand your living space. Organize your crafting recipes inside your storage lockers to make building easier.

## 🔍 Exploration Tips

Planet Zazura holds many secrets. Reach the CICADA crash site to uncover deep lore about the planet history. Always watch your oxygen levels when you explore caves. Use beacons to mark important locations so you do not get lost. Keep an eye on your proximity sensors to avoid aggressive Leviathans. If a creature spots you, swim toward tight spaces where larger animals cannot follow.

One player gathers materials while another pilot builds the base.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

If the game does not start, check for updates for your graphics card drivers. Right-click the game icon and select Run as Administrator if you see permission issues. Ensure that your antivirus software does not block the game files. If the game feels slow, lower your graphics settings in the menu. Turn off complex shadows or high reflection settings to improve your frame rate. Verify the game files if you experience crashes during gameplay.
